James Hupp

About

James is a person. He believes in being nice.

James leads content strategy at the U.S. Consumer Financial Protection Bureau. Right now, he's working mostly on a site refresh. (Check out our code!)

James's career began in digital campaigning. He started as an email strategist for non-profits. In 2008, he joined Obama for America to help grassroots supporters raise money. And in 2009, he helped a progressive digital startup get off the ground.

A campaign colleague asked James to join a different kind of startup in 2010. He was the lead writer as the CFPB launched its website. Campaigning had taught him to motivate an audience; government quickly taught him that information needs to be sustainable. He's been a content strategist since.

James is originally from Kansas City, Mo. Today, he spends time in both Washington and Brooklyn. His nascent international travel habit is abetted by his better-traveled girlfriend; his pickling habit is abetted, sadly, by nobody.
