About James

James is a content designer and strategist, currently managing the content design team at 18F. Previously, he worked at the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau. He is not a visual designer or a developer, as you can probably see from this website. But hopefully you can also see he likes trying.

James’s career began in digital campaigning: building email strategies for non-profits, managing a program and product that enabled Obama for America’s grassroots supporters to raise money before he knew what product management was, and helping a progressive digital startup get off the ground.

Campaigning taught him to focus on the audience; government taught him that information needs to be sustainable. He’s been putting both lessons to use in public service ever since.

James is originally from Kansas City, Mo. Today, he lives Brooklyn. His nascent international travel habit is abetted by his better-traveled girlfriend; his home fermenting habit is abetted, sadly, by nobody. He could really use a climbing buddy.
